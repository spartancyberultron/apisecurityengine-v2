Total Cost of Data Breach = (Direct Costs + Indirect Costs + Opportunity Costs) x Number of Records Breached

Categories Explained:

Direct Costs: Expenses directly associated with addressing the breach (investigation, remediation, legal fees, notifications, credit monitoring).

Indirect Costs: Costs that are not directly tied to the breach response but occur as a consequence (productivity loss, increased premiums, operational disruptions, customer loss, brand damage).

Opportunity Costs: Potential revenue or opportunities lost due to the breach's impact on the company's reputation and customer trust.
