Here's a breakdown of how this approach the calculation if there is a specific data:

  • Direct Costs: This would include the total sum of costs for incident investigation, remediation, legal expenses, notifications, and credit monitoring services.
  • Indirect Costs: This would sum up the loss of productivity, increased insurance premiums, operational disruptions, loss of customers/revenue, and damage to the brand.
  • Opportunity Costs: This would estimate the financial impact of lost business opportunities and the increased cost of acquiring new customers due to damaged trust.
  • Number of Records Breached: The total number of individual pieces of information compromised.
